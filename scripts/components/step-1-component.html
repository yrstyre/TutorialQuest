  • 1. Download Sublime Text 2 (or use other text editor of choice)
  • 2. Create a new folder named Quest. You can place it wherever you want
  • 3. Open Sublime Text 2. Choose View > Side Bar > Show Side bar
  • 4. Drag your new folder to Sublime and put it in your side bar
  • 5. In Sublime, right click on your project and choose "New File"
  • 6. Save the file, without writing anything in it, by pressing CTRL + S and name it to index.html
